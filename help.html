Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify a task?

Scan the beneficiary's QR code after they complete the task. You can then approve it from the "Task Approvals" page.

How are points calculated?

Physical tasks earn 20-30 points. Creative tasks earn 15-20 points. Complexity and time are factors.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all data is encrypted and stored locally on your device for this demo. Production versions use Blockchain.
